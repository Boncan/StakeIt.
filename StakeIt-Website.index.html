FAQ

Common questions.

Does money actually go to the Flat Earth Society?+
No. "Enemies" like the Pineapple Pizza Alliance and Flat Earth Society are fictional, humorous motivators. They exist to make the stakes feel personal and funny. All real penalty money (70%) goes to verified partner charities like Red Cross, Habitat for Humanity, World Wildlife Fund, and Feeding America. The remaining 30% supports the StakeIt platform.
Can I get my money back?+
Yes. Your unused wallet balance is always 100% withdrawable. If you load $50 and only use $20 worth of stakes, you can withdraw the remaining $30 at any time. The only money you can't get back is from penalties when you quit a session — that's the whole point.
Is this gambling?+
No. Gambling involves wagering on outcomes you can't control. StakeIt is a commitment device — you have 100% control over whether you complete your session. It's the same concept used in behavioral economics research at Yale and featured in Freakonomics. You're committing money to your own discipline, not betting on chance.
What happens if there's a real emergency?+
StakeIt includes an Emergency Exit option that charges only half the normal penalty. It's tracked separately in your analytics so you can see the difference between emergencies and regular quits. We understand life happens.
What data do you collect?+
Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. We don't store session details, payment card information, or personal data on any server. You can export all your data as a JSON file or delete everything at any time from the Profile screen.
Do I need to be 18+?+
Yes. StakeIt involves financial commitments, so we require all users to confirm they are 18 years or older during signup.
Which charities receive the donations?+
We partner with established 501(c)(3) organizations: American Red Cross, Habitat for Humanity, World Wildlife Fund, and Feeding America. Donations are batched and distributed quarterly to minimize transaction fees.
How is this different from StickK?+
StickK focuses on long-term commitment contracts (weeks/months). StakeIt is built for immediate focus sessions (10 minutes to 4 hours) with a fun, social-first design. Our viral sharing features, funny fictional enemies, and real-time timer create a completely different experience optimized for daily focus habits rather than long-term goal setting.
